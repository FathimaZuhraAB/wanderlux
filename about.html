Our Story

Who We Are

Passionate Travellers, Expert Planners

Founded in 2009 by two backpackers with a dream, WanderLux has grown into one of the world's most trusted travel companies. We believe every trip should be more than just a holiday — it should be a story you'll tell for the rest of your life.

From our humble beginnings organising small-group trips across Southeast Asia, we've expanded to cover over 200 destinations across 6 continents. Today, our team of 150 travel experts and local guides helps 50,000 travellers every year discover the world on their terms.

We are proud members of ABTA and ATOL, ensuring every trip is fully protected and every traveller is cared for — before, during, and after their journey.
