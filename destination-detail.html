Santorini, Greece

Perched dramatically on the rim of an ancient volcanic caldera, Santorini is one of the most iconic islands in the world. Famous for its whitewashed Cycladic architecture, sapphire-domed churches, and breathtaking sunsets over the Aegean Sea, this island paradise offers an unparalleled travel experience.

Our carefully curated 7-day package includes guided tours of Oia and Fira, a private sunset sailing cruise, wine tasting at local vineyards, and visits to the ancient ruins of Akrotiri. Whether you're a honeymooner, a solo adventurer, or a family seeking relaxation — Santorini has something magical for everyone.

D1

Arrival in Santorini — Welcome Dinner

Arrive at Santorini Airport, transfer to your luxury cave hotel in Oia. Evening welcome dinner overlooking the caldera.

D2

Oia Village Tour & Sunset Cruise

Morning guided walk through the famous blue-domed churches and narrow alleys of Oia. Evening private sunset sailing cruise.

D3

Akrotiri Ruins & Perissa Beach

Explore the preserved Minoan ruins of Akrotiri in the morning, then unwind at the famous black-sand Perissa Beach.

D4

Wine Tasting & Local Villages

Morning wine tasting at Santo Wines Winery with caldera views. Afternoon exploring the traditional villages of Pyrgos and Megalochori.

D5

Volcano & Hot Springs Boat Tour

Full day boat excursion to the active Nea Kameni volcano, swim in therapeutic hot springs, and visit Thirassia island.

D6

Greek Cooking Class & Free Afternoon

Morning hands-on Greek cooking class with a local chef. Free afternoon for shopping in Fira or optional helicopter tour.

D7

Departure Day

Leisurely breakfast, last-minute souvenir shopping, and transfer to Santorini Airport. Until next time, Greece!
